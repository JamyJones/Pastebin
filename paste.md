## Summary: Flashcards for Common Morning Interactions in Chinese

**Flashcard 1:**
---
**Front:** 你 (nǐ) 
**Pinyin:** nǐ

**Back:** 
**English Meaning:** You

**Example:** 
**Chinese:** 你好吗？
**Pinyin:** Nǐ hǎo ma?
**English:** How are you?

**Audio:** (nǐ)

**Engagement:** 
**Fill-in-the-Blank:** 
**你__？** (nǐ __ ma?) 
**Options:** 
- 好 (hǎo)
- 吗 (ma)
- 是 (shì)

---
**Flashcard 2:**

**Audio:** (zǎo shàng hǎo)

**Engagement:**

What is the English meaning of 早上好 (zǎo shàng hǎo)?
**Options:**

- Good Night
- Good Afternoon
