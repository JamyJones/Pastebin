## Flashcard 1: 你 (nǐ)  
---  
**Front:**  
- Chinese Character: **你**  
- Pinyin: **nǐ**  

**Back:**  
- English Meaning: **You**  

**Example Sentence:**  
- Chinese: **你好吗？**  
- Pinyin: **Nǐ hǎo ma?**  
- English Translation: **How are you?**  

**Audio:**  
- Pronunciation: **nǐ**  

**Engagement:**  
- Fill-in-the-Blank:  
  - **_____好吗？**
  - Possible Answers: (A) 你 (B) 他 (C) 她  
---
